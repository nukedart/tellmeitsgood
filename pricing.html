Common questions
What's the Triple Filter?
Every product is scored across 15 criteria in three gates: Gate 1 — Quality & Value, Gate 2 — Clean & Safe (ingredients, certifications), Gate 3 — Ethical Company (labor, sourcing, review practices). A product needs all three to earn the TELL ME IT'S GOOD badge.
What does the full breakdown actually show?
Each of the 15 criteria gets an individual score (1–10) with a specific evidence sentence and a source link — an actual URL to the review, study, or database we pulled from. It's the difference between a verdict and a receipt.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings — no questions, no friction. Your Pro access stays active until the end of the billing period.
What if a product I care about isn't in the database?
Free users can submit up to 2 new researches per day. Pro users get 25 — and can request "fresh research" on any existing product to get an updated verdict.
Is the Power tier available now?
Not yet — it's coming soon. If you're a brand or agency interested in competitor intelligence, email us and we'll let you know when it launches.
